Discord Community Rules

Be Respectful 📍

Treat all members with kindness and respect. No harassment, bullying, or toxic behavior will be tolerated.

No Spamming 📍

Avoid flooding chats with repetitive messages, unnecessary reactions, or links. Keep discussions clean and relevant.

Follow Channel Topics 📍

Stick to the topic of each channel. Off-topic posts or messages may be moved or deleted by moderators.

No Self-Promotion 📍

Promoting your own server, YouTube, or social media without permission is prohibited. Ask a mod before sharing links.

No NSFW Content 📍

Keep the server clean. Explicit or inappropriate content is not allowed in any form, including images, links, or messages.

No Cheating or Hacks 📍

Cheating or exploiting in games is strictly forbidden. Any member caught using cheats will be banned immediately.

Respect Privacy 📍

Don’t share anyone’s personal information without consent. This includes private messages (DMs) and real-life details.

No DM Advertising 📍

Messaging members with advertisements or promotions in DMs is strictly against the rules and will result in a ban.

Use Common Sense 📍

If something seems inappropriate or harmful, don’t do it. Mods have the final say in all disputes.

No Religious or Political Discussions: 📍

Do not engage in or share content that disrespects any religion, belief, or political ideology. Maintain a neutral and inclusive environment for everyone.

Follow Discord’s Terms of Service: 📍

Violating Discord’s TOS will result in removal from the server. Stay within the platform's rules and guidelines.

Final Decisions by Mods/Admins: 📍

Moderators and Admins hold authority to enforce rules. If you feel wrongly punished, only the @SoraPlays or @KnarliX may review and reverse decisions. Admins can also intervene in Moderator actions if needed.
